Family Protection Orders

Use this service to apply for a Family Law Protection Order from the Provincial Court of British Columbia. If you need a Family Law Protection Order from the Supreme Court of British Columbia instead, this service will not provide that for you.

Time: The average time to complete this service is 30min. If the Protection Order is urgent, you may get it the same day you file the application at a courthouse. A non-urgent Protection Order will take more time.

Cost: This service is free. The Government of British Columbia does not charge fees for Family Law cases in the Provincial Court of British Columbia.

Print: You will need a printer to print your application.

What you need to know:

Call 9-1-1 if you are in immediate danger.

What should I do if I'm not in immediate danger but am still afraid for my safety?
Victim Services can help you develop a personal safety plan.
