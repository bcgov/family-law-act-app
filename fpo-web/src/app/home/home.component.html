Family Law Act Protection Orders

Use this service to apply for a Protection Order under the Family Law Act from the Provincial Court of British Columbia. If you want a Protection Order from the Supreme Court of British Columbia instead of Provincial Court, this service cannot help you.

Call 9-1-1 if you are in immediate danger.

Please use a safer computer (for example, at a Public Library).

If you are not in immediate danger but are still afraid for your safety, VictimLinkBC can help you develop a personal safety plan.
Call: 1-800-563-0808 | TTY: 604-875-0885 | Text: 604-863-6381


Cost: This service is free. The Government of British Columbia does not charge fees for Family Law cases in the Provincial Court of British Columbia.

Time: The average time to complete this service is 30-45 minutes. At this time, will need to complete your application in one session. This service will not save your information. If a judge decides that your situation is urgent, they may hear your application the same day you file it.

Print: You will need to print your application to file it at a Provincial Courthouse. At this time, you cannot use this service to file electronically.
